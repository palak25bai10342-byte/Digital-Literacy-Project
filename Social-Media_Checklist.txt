SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DONT’S
DO’S
? Keep your profile clean and professional 
? Use respectful and polite language in posts and comments 
? Verify information before sharing anything 
? Adjust privacy settings to protect your personal data 
? Think before posting anything online

DONT’S
? Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly 
? Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
? Don’t engage in online arguments or negativity 
? Don’t spread fake news or unverified information 
? Don’t misuse someone else’s photos or content
