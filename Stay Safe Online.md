Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Verify the identity of the sender before making transactions  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Regularly check your bank statements  
- Do not install apps from unknown sources  

Financial Safety Tips:
- Always double-check UPI IDs before sending money  
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN  

Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
